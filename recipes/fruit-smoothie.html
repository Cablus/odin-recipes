Fruit Smoothie

Description

The fruit smoothie is a cold, creamy, and naturally sweet drink made from blended fresh fruits, yogurt, and milk or juice. It’s packed with vitamins, fiber, and energy — making it a perfect choice for breakfast, post-workout fuel, or a healthy mid-day snack. Easy to customize and quick to prepare, it’s both delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Mango chunks
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the banana.
  2. Wash and cut the strawberries and mango.
  3. Add all fruits to a blender.
  4. Pour in yogurt and milk.
  5. Add honey and ice cubes if desired.
  6. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  7. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
